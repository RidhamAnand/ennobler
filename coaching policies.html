Ennobler Private Limited, Coaching Policies

At Ennobler Private Limited, we prioritize the confidentiality and privacy of our clients. We commit to maintaining the strictest confidentiality standards to safeguard all client information shared during the coaching process.
We ensure that all personal and business-related information disclosed by clients remains confidential and will not be shared with any third parties without explicit consent, except in cases where required by law.
Confidentiality Policy: At Ennobler Private Limited, we prioritize the confidentiality and privacy of our clients. We commit to maintaining the strictest confidentiality standards to safeguard all client information shared during the coaching process. We ensure that all personal and business-related information disclosed by clients remains confidential and will not be shared with any third parties without explicit consent, except in cases where required by law.

Service Agreement: Our coaching services are provided based on a mutually agreed-upon service agreement between Ennobler Private Limited and the client. The service agreement outlines the scope of work, coaching objectives, session duration, and the agreed-upon fees for the coaching services. The responsibilities and expectations of both the client and the coach are clearly defined in the service agreement to establish a productive coaching relationship.

Code of Ethics: Ennobler Private Limited adheres to the highest professional standards and ethics in executive coaching. Our coaching practices align with the ethical guidelines set forth by recognized coaching associations, such as the International Coach Federation (ICF) and the Association for Coaching (AC). We maintain integrity, confidentiality, and respect for our clients' autonomy throughout the coaching process.

Payment and Refund Policy: Ennobler Private Limited specifies its accepted payment methods and the terms of payment in the service agreement. Clients are responsible for making timely payments as agreed upon in the service agreement. In case of cancellations or rescheduling, clients are required to provide a minimum notice period as outlined in the service agreement. Refund policies, if applicable, will be clearly communicated in the service agreement.

Scheduling and Rescheduling: Clients can schedule coaching sessions through our online booking system or by contacting Ennobler Private Limited directly. To ensure a smooth coaching process, clients are encouraged to schedule sessions in advance. Any requests for rescheduling or cancellations should be made within the agreed-upon notice period, as specified in the service agreement

Communication Policy: Ennobler Private Limited maintains open and transparent communication channels with our clients. Clients can reach us via email or phone during specified working hours. We strive to respond to client communication in a timely manner, taking into account the nature and urgency of the inquiry.

Intellectual Property: All materials, tools, and resources provided during coaching sessions are the intellectual property of Ennobler Private Limited. Clients are granted a limited, non-exclusive license to use the coaching materials for personal and professional development purposes only. The reproduction, distribution, or sharing of coaching materials outside the coaching relationship requires prior written consent from Ennobler Private Limited.
Limitation of Liability: Ennobler Private Limited provides coaching services to support clients in their personal and professional growth. However, clients retain full responsibility for their actions, decisions, and outcomes resulting from the coaching process. Ennobler Private Limited cannot be held liable for any direct or indirect damages or losses arising from the coaching services or their implementation.
Testimonials and Case Studies: Ennobler Private Limited may seek client permission to use testimonials or case studies for promotional purposes, ensuring that client identity and confidentiality are preserved. Client consent is obtained before using any names, identifying information, or testimonials on our website or other marketing materials.

These policies are designed to provide transparency, protect client interests, and establish a foundation for a successful coaching engagement. It is advisable to review and tailor these policies to your specific needs and consult with legal professionals to ensure compliance with local laws and regulations.
